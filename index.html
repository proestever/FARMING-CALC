How To Get The Numbers

To calculate your potential returns more accurately, follow these steps:

  1. Visit Gopulse.com and click on the 30-day price range to obtain the appreciation rates for PLSX and INC. If these values remain constant, they will help you calculate your return more easily.
  2. Go to app.Pulsex.com, click on "EARN" -> "Farms", and find the PLSX/INC PAIR.
  3. Enter the same dollar amount you used in this calculator into the PLSX/INC pair section, select a 30-day period, and the platform will generate your estimated INC yield for that period.
  4. Use the provided INC yield value in the "Monthly INC Yield (tokens)" section of this calculator to get your results.
